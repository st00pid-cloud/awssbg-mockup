Mission and Vision Events and Initiatives

Built from the ground up in the Cordilleras.

The Jaguars are the premier cloud-focused student organization at UC — an incubator for practical technical expertise, leadership growth, and open-source collaboration. We break out of standard curricula to get hands-on with AWS infrastructure, build multi-agentic AI systems, design secure networks, and prepare for globally recognized certifications.


Our vision: establish the University of the Cordilleras as a regional hub of excellence for cloud-native engineering and ethical AI development within CAR — producing high-caliber builders ready for complex global infrastructure challenges.
